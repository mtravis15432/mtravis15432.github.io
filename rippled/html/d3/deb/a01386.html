Detailed Description

template<class Mutex>
class beast::SharedMutexAdapter< Mutex >

Adapts a regular Lockable to conform to the SharedMutex concept. Shared locks become unique locks with this interface. Two threads may not simultaneously acquire ownership of the lock. Typically the Mutex template parameter will be a CriticalSection.
